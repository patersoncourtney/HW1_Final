Top Worldwide Destinations

I have been fortunate to have been able to travel extensively. I wanted to share with you some of my most favorite destinations, and what they have to offer.

Israel

  • Fantastic food, especially shakshuka
  • Hot, desert weather
  • Surprisingly very safe

Japan
  • Sushi that is to die for
  • Respectful, peaceful culture
  • Scenic hiking trails

Italy
  • Jovial people
  • Endless pasta
  • Incredible views
