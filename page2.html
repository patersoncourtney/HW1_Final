Israel

One of the safest, friendliest places in the world!

I travelled to Tel Aviv, Israel in May for a legal negotiations course through Northwestern Law School. A lot of people think that Israel is unsafe given recent conflict with neighboring countries. However, it was the safest I felt travelling in a really long time. The people were warm, inviting, and respectful, and the food was remarkable. I especially enjoyed the beaches during 95 degree weather!
